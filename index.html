Client: apricot tote-bags

Description

The purpose of this website is to be an e-Commerce tote-bag website. The website will feature personalized screen printed tote-bags and certain pages for the customer to interact with.

Competitive Analysis

  • https://pacifictotecompany.com/: Colorful website design, Shop, About Us, Films, More (navbar), Search, About Us, FAQ (footer)
  • https://society6.com/: Sell all kinds of custom items, including tote bags (all are designed by artists) A lot of options in the navbar (I want to limit mine)
  • https://www.etsy.com/: Similar to Society6, sell a lot of custom items made by others

Audience

Persona 1

  • Demographics: 20 year old art student, has a job but doesn't make a lot of money
  • Lifestyle: busy with classes and extracurriculars, travels a lot, loves fashion
  • Needs: needs something to cary their materials but backpacks are too big and purses are too small, affortable clothes and items, support other artists
  • Preferences: shops mostly online and on phone, unique and unconventional items

Persona 2

  • Demographics: 35 year old entreprenuer, lives in a big city, middle class income
  • Lifestyle: busy running around to meetings, travels a lot, loves quality items
  • Needs: needs something to cary their materials that is beautiful but also functional, wants unique items
  • Preferences: shops mostly online and on phone
